🗣 Talking Points — Why Jason?

  • Jason is running as a responsible alternative to political chaos on both sides — disciplined, lawful, and serious about governing.
  • He refuses monetary donations from large donors or PACs — so he answers only to his constituents, not special interests.
  • He supports real fiscal discipline: no wasteful governance, no reckless spending, and no executive orders designed to fail in court.
  • He's tough on housing costs — calling out private equity firms that are pricing working families out of their own neighborhoods.
  • He believes in a diversified, domestic energy future — nuclear, solar, wind — that strengthens national security and competes with China.
  • He stands for professional, constitutional law enforcement — at the border and in Washington, where no one should be above the law.
  • Pennsylvania's 14th District deserves a candidate who "does the work" — not one who makes headlines and governs without integrity.

Simple Ways to Help Get the Word Out

📱 Share on Social Media

Follow and share Jason's posts on Facebook, Instagram, X, and YouTube. Tag friends and family in Southwest Pennsylvania. Engagement grows reach — likes, shares, and comments all help.

💬 Talk to Your Neighbors

Word of mouth is the most powerful campaign tool there is. Tell one person today why you support Jason. Ask them to tell one more. That's how movements start.

📧 Email Your Network

Send a short email to friends and family in the district. Point them to this website. Let them know there's a serious, principled candidate in this race — and the deadline to register to vote.

🏘 Community Events

Carry a clipboard to local events, churches, sports games, or neighborhood gatherings. Have a short conversation. You don't have to give a speech — just share what you believe in.
