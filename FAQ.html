💰 Responsible Governance & Fiscal Discipline

  • Principled Leadership: Rejects political extremes in favor of responsible governance, fiscal discipline, the rule of law, and moral clarity.
  • Effective Fiscal Policy: Prioritizes "doing the work" through proper, legal channels — avoiding wasteful legal fees and back pay caused by overturned executive orders.
  • Disciplined Spending: Commits to not spending money the government does not have; stabilizes the cost of living through precision and coordination rather than "move fast and break things" slogans.
  • Smart Global Trade: Maintains and leverages existing global trade infrastructure to put American workers first — through targeted reshoring of critical supply chains and renegotiating bad deals.
  • American Competitiveness: Competes on quality, innovation, and productivity — not walls that ultimately raise prices for our own families.

🏠 Housing & Cost of Living

  • Combating Market Distortion: Addresses the distortion caused by private equity firms buying up entire zip codes and pricing working families out of their own neighborhoods.
  • Antitrust Enforcement in Housing: Calls for revision of policies on private equity-owned housing and firm enforcement of competition law — protecting the American Dream of homeownership.
  • Private Property, Not Corporate Monopoly: Believes in private property rights while opposing billion-dollar funds competing against families for starter homes.

⚡ Energy Independence & Innovation

  • Diversified Energy Portfolio: Supports a rational-investor approach to energy — nuclear, solar, wind, and alternatives — to ensure national security and meet the explosive demands of AI and data centers.
  • Nuclear Expansion: Promotes nuclear power as the gold standard for clean, powerful, and scalable energy density; modern reactor designs are safer and more buildable than ever.
  • Competitive Energy Economy: Prioritizes aggressive domestic investment in diversified energy production and battery storage to compete with China — refusing to cede the energy economy.
  • Powering The Future: Ensures reliable, affordable, domestic energy to meet the massive power demands of the emerging AI and data center economy.

⚖️ Law, Order & Accountability

  • Professional Border Enforcement: Supports rigorous, professional, and accountable border enforcement that adheres to constitutional rights and proper background checks — not quota-driven shortcuts.
  • Structural Accountability: Advocates for transparency and vigorous oversight to break the entrenchment of bureaucracies and incumbents on Capitol Hill — on both sides of the aisle.
  • No One Above the Law: Opposes insider trading protections and immunity for corruption for government officials; public office demands a higher moral and legal standard, period.

🏛️ Core Values

  • Judeo-Christian Foundations: Rooted in the dignity of every person, the sanctity of family, and our responsibility to one another and our communities.
  • Moral Seriousness: Rejects identity politics and divisive buzzwords in favor of governance that actually reflects its stated principles — holding leaders to the same standard on Monday that they preach on Sunday.
  • Right vs. Wrong: Calls for a party that is as moral as it is strong, and as principled as it is populist — one that does the work.
"I am asking for your vote — not because I belong to the right team, but because I intend to do the work. Responsibly. Accountably. And Lawfully."
